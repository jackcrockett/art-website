Founder - Team Principal

Jack Crockett

Based Adelaide, SA
Platform iRacing
Focus Endurance / GT

ART was built from a simple belief: that sim racing deserves to be taken seriously. Not as a casual hobby, but as a competitive discipline with real structure, real accountability, and real results.

As Team Principal, the mandate is clear - build a team that competes with the same professionalism you'd expect from a real-world motorsport operation. That means performance-based pay structures, disciplined driver selection, and a long-term growth strategy that doesn't compromise on standards.

The Aventa brand represents more than a racing team. It's a vision for what sim racing can become when the people running it treat it like it matters.

Behind the Team

Structure that supports speed.

A racing team is more than its drivers. ART is structured to support competitive performance at every level.

Driver Development

ART fosters a culture of collective improvement. Drivers share data, debrief together, and push each other - the team gets faster as a unit, not just as individuals.

Race Strategy

Pit window timing, tyre management, fuel strategy. Endurance racing is won in the pits as much as on track.

Team Culture

Accountability, communication, and standards that don't drop. ART is built on the belief that how you show up in the team matters as much as how you show up on track.

Content & Media

Race highlights, driver profiles, team announcements. ART builds its profile alongside its results.
