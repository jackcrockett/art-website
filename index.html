The ART Philosophy

Sim racing deserves to be taken seriously. Not as a pastime, but as a discipline, one that rewards preparation, rewards precision, and rewards the drivers who hold themselves to a genuine standard.

ART was built around that belief. We compete with intent, we recruit with purpose, and we race with the kind of integrity that makes results mean something. Culture isn't a buzzword here - it's the filter every decision gets run through.

How We Operate

Three things
that don't move.

01

Performance First

Compensation is tied to results. Drivers who deliver get rewarded. There is no salary for showing up - there is only payout for performance. That keeps the standard where it belongs.

02

Two Teams. One Standard.

ART is the primary esports operation - application only, built for drivers who are ready to compete at the highest level. For those earlier in their journey, Aventa Garage Racing Team (AGRT) is the development and community team - open to the wider sim racing community, and the pathway into the ART ecosystem.

03

Long-Term Build

This isn't a season project. ART is structured to grow - in drivers, in results, and in reputation. We're building something that competes seriously today and scales into something recognised globally.
